Profesional Profile

My name is George Trendafilov and I am a web developer. I love to code because the things I create make a difference in people’s lives. I also love to code because it is a team effort and it is amazing when things come together to form a useful product or tool.

For the last 8 years, I have worked in the Digital marketing industry developing and executing acquisition and retention campaigns. My previous roles include managing and evolving the email marketing strategy and overseeing the operational aspects of campaign deployment. I have hands-on experience with SEO, Google AdWords and Google Analytics. My responsibilities included updating email and landing page content using HTML and CSS. These activities evolved into passion for coding and development.

Following my passion, I enrolled at LearnigFuze an accelerated web development program. I quickly realized that this was one of the best decisions I have ever made. I had the opportunity to work with the latest front and back end technologies. My knowledge of JavaScript, HTML, CSS and React increased tremendously as I was able to contribute to the development of robust applications.

I strongly believe that my digital marketing experience combined with the web development knowledge can contribute to the success of your team and the company as a whole. I am excited about the opportunity to join your company and bring my passion and dedication for coding and development.
